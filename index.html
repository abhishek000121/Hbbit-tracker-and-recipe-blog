Paneer Butter Masala

By Abhishek | July 22, 2025

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 3 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Cashew Nuts
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • ½ tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • ¼ cup Fresh Cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and sauté onion, ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and cashews. Blend to smooth puree.
  2. In another pan, heat butter and cook the puree. Add spices and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add paneer cubes and mix well. Stir in cream and crushed Kasuri methi. Simmer for 2–3 minutes.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot with Butter Naan, Garlic Roti, or Jeera Rice.

Tips

  • Use tofu for a vegan version.
  • For richer taste, increase cream quantity.
